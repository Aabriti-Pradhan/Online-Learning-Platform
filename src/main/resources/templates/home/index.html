About us

PrepHunt is your all-in-one study hub, designed to make learning organized, efficient, and engaging. It allows students to access course materials in a structured platform, take notes while tagging topics and subtopics for easy revision, and test themselves using AI-generated quizzes or tests created by teachers and peers. By consolidating scattered study materials into one unified system, PrepHunt helps students stay focused, track their learning progress, and prepare effectively for exams.

More about PrepHunt

Beyond individual learning, PrepHunt encourages collaboration and motivation. Students can connect with friends, share resources, and participate in tests to challenge themselves in a supportive environment. A gamified ranking system keeps learners motivated by tracking test activity and performance, while teachers can effortlessly upload content, monitor progress, and manage classroom performance, all within a single platform. PrepHunt transforms traditional study habits into a structured, interactive, and results-driven learning experience.
